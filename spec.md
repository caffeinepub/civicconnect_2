# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Expand the sub-service lists for 7 service category cards on the Services page with their complete, exact item lists.

**Planned changes:**
- Update FSSAI License card with 5 sub-service items: FSSAI Registration, FSSAI State License, FSSAI Central License, FPO Mark Certification, FSSAI Annual Return Filing
- Update Trademark Registration card with 7 sub-service items: Trademark Registration, Trademark Objection / Opposition Reply, Trademark Hearing, Brand Name Registration, Design Registration, Copyright Registration, Patent Registration
- Update Pollution Control Board card with 10 sub-service items including CGWA Water Boring, CTE / CTO Certificate, EPR Registration, Plastic Waste Authorization, E-Waste Recycling Authorization, Biomedical Waste License, Hazardous Waste Management NOC, Solid Waste Management Authorization, Battery Waste Management, Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Import Pollution NOC
- Update Tender Services card with 10 sub-service items including GeM Registration, Vendor Assessment, Vendor Assessment OEM, Catalogue Upload on GeM, IREPS Registration, E-Procurement Registration, State Procurement Portal Registration, MSTC / E-Auction Portal Registration, Coal E-Auction Service, Tender Submission Service
- Update Digital Signature Certificate card with 5 sub-service items: Digital Signature (Organization), Digital Signature (Signing), Digital Signature (Signing + Encryption), DGFT Digital Signature, NRI Digital Signature
- Update Agreement & Contract card with 7 sub-service items: Non Disclosure Agreement, Partnership Deed, Residential Rent Agreement, Joint Venture Agreement, Commercial Rent Agreement, Affidavits, LLP Deed
- Update Small Business Support Service card with 7 sub-service items: Website Design, Logo Maker, Billing Software, Letter Pad Design, Visiting Card Design, Product Booklet, Business Booklet
- Ensure all sub-service items are fully visible with no truncation, hiding, or "show more" collapse
- Card heights adjust dynamically to fit all items with no empty whitespace
- All other 8 service cards, page layout, grid, icons, and styling remain unchanged

**User-visible outcome:** All 7 updated service cards on the Services page display their complete sub-service lists, fully expanded and visible, with card heights fitting the content naturally.
